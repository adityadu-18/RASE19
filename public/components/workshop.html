About

This workshop is aimed to familiarize the participants with the field of robotics. The workshop includes a brief theory and practical session on line follower, edge detector, sumo wrestler and Android Smartphone controlled robot. The event is coordinated and conducted by Amrita Club for Robotics and Mechatronics (ACROM) & IEEE RAS, Amrita School of Engineering, Bangalore. Participants are provided with take away robotic kit and event is certified by IEEE & ACROM.
